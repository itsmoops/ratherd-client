About

Unless you had a terrible childhood, you already know how to play! Terrible childhood aside, here's some info about this site:

Play

  • Every time you choose a Rather, you are voting.
  • Community votes affect matches:
    • Each Rather has a win percentage.
    • The first Rather selected for a match is completely random.
    • The second Rather selected will have the closest possible win percentage to the competing Rather (Hopefully creating a good matchup).
  • Use the This Sucks button ESPECIALLY for Rathers that don't make any sense. You won't see that shit anymore.

Submit

  • Create an account and log in to start submitting your own Rathers.
  • A prompt is given on the Submit page. Your Rather does NOT need to be related to the prompt:
    • A Rather works best when it starts with a verb in the present tense (walk, jump, murder, etc.). Just use your best judgment, k?
  • See if you can create the most Rather'd Rather! You probably won't, but give it a shot!

Track

  • View which Rathers are the most popular/controversial within the community.
  • Keep an eye on your own submitted Rathers on your user page:
    • Rathers that have been This Sucks'd enough, will become deactivated for the community.
    • Your user page is where you will see if one of your Rathers has been de-activated (Sucks bro).
